How do I get started with feecollec?
Getting started is easy. Schedule a free demo on our website and our team will guide you through setup, data import, and go-live — typically within one week.
Do I have to pay for the demo?
No — all first demos are completely free. We'll review your current workflow and show you exactly how feecollec fits your operations.
Which payment gateways does feecollec support?
We integrate with major Malaysian payment gateways: Billplz, GHL (eGHL), FPX, iPay88, Touch 'n Go eWallet, and Payex. Need a specific gateway? Talk to us about custom integrations.
Can feecollec handle multiple properties?
Yes. feecollec is built for multi-property management. Manage all your JMBs, MCs, and developments from a single account with role-based access for each property team.
